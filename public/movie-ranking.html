Mitch's Favorite Movies

My top 3 movies of all time.

The Sandlot

This movie perfectly illustrates what living in 1950s California summer may be like for a 13 year old. He wants nothing but to be accepted by his neigborhood... and to play ball.

American Beauty

A gross, beautiful, shocking thriller/drama starring the gross man. Chilling end to a perfectly shot movie.

Rounders

Why must to spend an entire year of pain and fear just to learn a lesson of why you shouldn't trust people or gamble with opportunities.
